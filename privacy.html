Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026

p>

MyPlanner is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the MyPlanner mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MyPlanner stores all your personal data locally on your device using Apple SwiftData framework. When you enable iCloud sync, this data is stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. AI Features and Third-Party Data Processing

MyPlanner offers optional AI-powered features that use the Groq AI service (Llama 3.3 model). When you use these features, relevant text you enter (task descriptions, notes, diary entries) is sent to Groq servers for processing.

Important: Before any data is sent to Groq, the app explicitly asks for your permission. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in Settings. Groq processes the data and does not store it after processing.

3. On-Device Processing

Some features use Apple on-device NaturalLanguage framework. This data never leaves your device.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Subscriptions are processed through Apple App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and optionally in your iCloud account. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. All data transmission to AI services is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except for AI processing when you explicitly opt in.

7. Children Privacy

MyPlanner is not directed to children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

9. Contact Us

Contact us at: myplanner.app.support@gmail.com
